About Nexus

Nexus is a Web development club for Technology enthusiasts. Objective of this forum is self learning perspective to web development. we intend to understand the basic concepts of various technologies and further more implement them in real time. We aim at gaining more and more expertise in these emerging technologies and there after grabbing different job opportunities in the various fields. Main moto Of nexus is to bring Developers together!

Activities

Expert Sessions

The club will be conducting Expert Sessions for the fellow members. External Faculties or working personnel's will be invited to deliver sessions on web technology. We are all here to learn and explore this field of technology so everyone can suggest Experts who are ready to share their set of skills.

Weekly Tasks

All the fellow members will be given the study material and the team will give tasks based on the learning material provided. These tasks will be having a particular deadline which is to be followed by all the members. These tasks will be then evaluated by our fellow members who have an hand over that particular field of interest. This will happen on a monthly basis. So everyone can solve their doubts together with discussions.

Internship Opportunities

Internship opportunities from an upcoming IT company are available. The Club will be signing an MOU with Webify Solutions Pvt Ltd. The company is hiring qualified interns. Individuals with a good set of skill set may avail this opportunity. Interviews will be conducted for those who portray their skills as per requirement.
